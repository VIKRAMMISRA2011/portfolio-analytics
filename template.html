07

Methodology & data coverage

  1. Sources. Zerodha tradebook + P&L + fund ledger (FY Sep '23–Aug '24) and current holdings; AngelOne trades + P&L (Oct '24–Aug '25); Groww order history, two P&L reports, dividend report and full fund ledger (May '24–Jun '26).
  2. Trade reconstruction. Trades matched into lots with FIFO. Validation: the rebuilt Zerodha realized P&L equals the broker-stated figure to within ₹0.004; unmatched quantities all trace to corporate actions (Reliance 1:1 bonus, Siemens demerger, rights entitlements, IPO allotments), which are deferred to broker-adjusted statements.
  3. Ledger forensics. Groww's settlement ledger closes against deposits, withdrawals and charges within ₹40. A ₹185,516 settlement gap identified the OLA IPO application (2,441 × ₹76), recovering a +₹59,234 realized gain absent from the broker's own P&L because IPO cost bases are excluded there.
  4. Charges. All totals are net of broker charges (₹27.2k). For the monthly curve, charges are allocated pro-rata to months by realized activity; KPI totals use exact statement values.
  5. Exclusions, disclosed. One Zerodha exit (LODHA, 26 sh, cost ₹38,565) occurred in a period not covered by provided ledgers; its realized P&L is excluded from every total on this page. AngelOne's fund ledger and OLA Electric's current mark are pending — the affected metrics display PENDING rather than estimates.
  6. Separation principle. Realized record and open book are never blended. The all-in XIRR, where shown, includes every cash flow and the marked value of all open positions; it is reported with its drivers rather than omitted when unflattering.
